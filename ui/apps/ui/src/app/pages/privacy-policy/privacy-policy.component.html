Privacy Policy of EOSC User Dashboard

The EOSC User Dashboard platform, URL: my.eosc-portal.eu, (hereinafter referred to as "UD" or "Application") abides by the following principles when handling personal information:

  1. We collect a small amount of personal information about our users in order to provide them with our content, products and services;
  2. We limit the sharing or disclosure of this personal information to our needs or to comply with applicable legal requirements;
  3. We give users meaningful choices over the use of their personal information;
  4. We strive to protect the personal information that we hold.

This Privacy Notice describes the policies applied with respect to the personal information collected through the UD, or when the UD communicates with its users ("you" or "user"), and the choices that are made available to them. "Personal Information" means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.

The EOSC User Dashboard is a visual display of your personal website space. Each User can define and design his own scientific profile and - based on it - receive resources tailored to their needs. Among others, User Dashboard includes: the user profile, settings, personalized content, user space access and feedback mechanisms. Recommended resources are provided via EOSC Marketplace website.

The project is co-funded by the European Commission, therefore the EU data processing law applies. The policy on "protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data by the Community institutions" is based on EU regulation as described in EC Legal notice on personal data protection.

Your use of the UD signifies that you agree to all terms of this Privacy Notice. 3. What information we collect, and how we collect it.

The Application manages different types of data, all in compliance with the current European legislation on Data Protection. Any Data concerning the User is collected to enable the EGI Foundation to provide its services, as well as for the following purposes: tag management, displaying content from external platforms, analytics, contacting the user, managing contacts and sending messages, Interaction with data collection platforms and other third parties. Among the types of Personal Data that this Application collects, by itself or through third parties, are: cookies, usage data, first name, last name, email address, various types of data including User affiliation and country.

Data voluntarily supplied by the User - The UD is designed for registered users only, which means it requires some personal information. The data collected and further processed are necessary to access the Application, as well as for communication and follow-up activities. Said consent may be revoked at any time, whereby the ability to use the service in question ceases.

EOSC Portal Marketplace Resource Information - The main entity managed by the Marketplace portal is a resource. A resource is described by a well-defined schema, which contains:

  1. basic information about the resource , such as the resource ID, name, description, provider, etc.,
  2. marketing information related to the target users, multimedia related to the resource, etc.
  3. classification information, such as the category and the scientific domain related to the resource,
  4. location information, i.e. countries and languages the resource is available,
  5. contact information, i.e. public contact details for the person who is responsible for replying to user requests for this service,
  6. maturity information, i.e. TRL, version, phase , etc.,
  7. dependencies to other EOSC resources,
  8. attribution to funding bodies and projects,
  9. management information, i.e. terms of use, helpdesk and other operational information,
  10. access and order information,
  11. financial information, such as price or financial model.

EOSC User Dashboard usage activity and user-collected information - Users of the platform are authenticated end-users who access and browse the UD. Usage events and statistics are collected by the Application and then analyzed to produce consolidated statistics and recommendations for related searches for the user.

The following information about user visits is collected in the EOSC User Dashboard database:

  1. the user unique identifier,
  2. first name,
  3. last name,
  4. email,
  5. reason for accessing EOSC services,
  6. country of origin,
  7. customer typology, i.e., researcher, citizen, educator, provider, etc.,
  8. organization/company,
  9. country of collaboration,
  10. webpage of the EOSC portal accessed,
  11. ordered services,
  12. messages (communication with support team),
  13. user rating information

Users are responsible for any third-party Personal Data obtained, published or shared through this Application and confirm that they have the third party's consent to provide the Data to the EGI Foundation.

Cookies - Any cookies or other tracking tools used by this Application, or by the owners of third-party services used through this Application, serve the purpose of providing the service required by the User, in addition to any other purposes described in the present document and in the Cookie Policy, if available.

6. How we use this information

The Application uses personal information for the following:

  1. Fulfillment of requests - We may use your personal information to deal with your inquiries, register you to our events, and send you the publications or documents that you request.
  2. Personalized recommendations - the Application automatically collects and stores certain types of information about User’s activity in EOSC Portal, including your interaction with provided resources and services available through the Website. Such processing includes ‘profiling’ that consists of automated processing of personal data evaluating your scientific interests and activity on the Website - in order to predict what resources will be the most interesting for a specific User.
  3. Internal business purposes - We may use the collected information for internal business purposes, such as for audits or to track attendance at events.
  4. Site operation - We use cookies to assign a unique identifier to a user's computer.
  5. Statistical analysis - We use aggregated data about UD usage (which do not identify a specific user), such as the number of users who have visited certain pages of the UD, or how long users are spending on a particular page, in order to develop statistics as to the use of the SUD, so that we can understand how users interact with the UD, to improve its content, products, or services.
  6. Displaying content from external platforms.
